I. Tng Quan

1. Gii thiu v ng dng

  • L thuyt: My in mt chiu (DC Machines) l loi my in c lch s pht trin lu i v vn gi vai tr quan trng trong nhiu lnh vc. Chng c th hot ng ch my pht (bin c nng thnh in nng mt chiu) hoc ng c (bin in nng mt chiu thnh c nng). Cc ng dng tiu biu bao gm:
    • ng c: Xe in (xe golf, xe nng), thang my, cn cu, my cn thp, cc h thng truyn ng i hi iu khin tc v moment chnh xc (nh trong robot cng nghip, my cng c CNC trc y), ng c khi ng t,...
    • My pht: Cc ngun in mt chiu c lp, my pht kch t cho my pht in ng b ln,...

2. Cu to c bn

  • L thuyt: My in DC in hnh gm cc b phn chnh sau:
    • Stator (Phn tnh / Phn cm):
      • V my (Frame/Yoke): Thng lm bng thp c hoc thp tm, l khung chu lc v to thnh mch dn t cho t thng kch t.
      • Cc t chnh (Main Pole): Gn vo v my, gm li thp cc t v dy qun kch t (Field Winding). Dy qun kch t to ra t trng chnh ($\Phi_f$) ca my khi c dng in chy qua.
      • Cc t ph (Interpole - nu c): Nm gia cc cc t chnh, gip ci thin qu trnh i chiu dng in c gp.
      • Cun b (Compensating Winding - nu c): t trong cc rnh trn b mt cc t chnh, gip gim nh hng ca phn ng phn ng.
    • Rotor (Phn quay / Phn ng):
      • Li st phn ng (Armature Core): Ghp t cc l thp k thut in mng, cch in vi nhau gim tn hao do dng xoy. Li st c cc rnh dc trc t dy qun phn ng.
      • Dy qun phn ng (Armature Winding): Gm nhiu phn t dy qun ni vi nhau theo quy lut nht nh v ni ra cc phin gp ca c gp. y l ni sc in ng c cm ng (my pht) hoc ni dng in chy qua to ra moment quay (ng c).
      • C gp (Commutator): Gm nhiu phin ng (lam ng) hnh r qut, ghp thnh hnh tr v cch in vi nhau cng nh vi trc. Cc u dy ca phn t dy qun phn ng c hn vo cc phin gp tng ng.
      • Trc my (Shaft): Truyn chuyn ng quay.
    • C cu chi than (Brush Assembly):
      • Chi than (Carbon Brush): Thng lm bng carbon graphite, c gi trong gi chi than v t ln b mt c gp nh l xo. Chi than ng yn so vi v my, lm nhim v dn in t mch ngoi vo/ra dy qun phn ng ang quay thng qua c gp.

3. Nguyn l in t c bn

y l hai nh lut vt l nn tng gii thch hot ng ca my in.

a. Lc in t (Nguyn l ng c):

  • L thuyt: Khi mt dy dn c chiu di hiu dng $l$ (phn nm trong t trng) mang dng in $I$ c t trong mt t trng u c cm ng t $B$, n s chu tc dng ca mt lc in t $\vec{F}_e$. Chiu ca lc ny tun theo Quy tc bn tay tri: t bn tay tri sao cho ng sc t ($\vec{B}$) xuyn vo lng bn tay, chiu t c tay n ngn tay gia ch chiu dng in ($\vec{I}$), th ngn tay ci choi ra 90 ch chiu ca lc in t ($\vec{F}_e$).
  • Cng thc: Vect lc c xc nh bi tch c hng: $$ \vec{F}_e = I (\vec{l} \times \vec{B}) $$ ln ca lc c tnh bng: $$ F_e = I l B \sin(\alpha) $$ Trong :
    • $F_e$: ln lc in t (N)
    • $I$: Cng dng in chy qua dy dn (A)
    • $l$: Chiu di hiu dng ca dy dn trong t trng (m)
    • $B$: Cm ng t ca t trng (T - Tesla)
    • $\alpha$: Gc hp bi vect chiu di $\vec{l}$ (theo chiu dng in) v vect cm ng t $\vec{B}$. Lc t gi tr cc

import math

def tinh_luc_dien_tu(I, l, B, alpha_deg=90.0):
  """
  Tnh  ln lc in t tc dng ln dy dn.

  Args:
    I: Cng  dng in (A).
    l: Chiu di phn dy dn trong t trng (m).
    B: Cm ng t (T - Tesla).
    alpha_deg: Gc gia dy dn v t trng (), mc nh l 90.

  Returns:
     ln lc in t (N) hoc None nu li.
  """
  if l <= 0 or B < 0:
      print("Li: Chiu di (l) phi > 0 v Cm ng t (B) phi >= 0.")
      return None
  alpha_rad = math.radians(alpha_deg)
  #  ln lc khng ph thuc du ca I, ch ph thuc gi tr tuyt i
  Fe = abs(I) * l * B * math.sin(alpha_rad)
  print(f"   [Tnh Fe]: I={I:.2f}A, l={l:.2f}m, B={B:.2f}T, alpha={alpha_deg}deg => Fe = {Fe:.3f} N")
  return Fe

5. Nguyn l hot ng ng c DC

  • To Moment quay: Hot ng ca ng c DC da trn nguyn l lc in t. Khi t mt in p mt chiu $V_t$ vo hai u chi than, dng in $I_a$ s chy t chi than dng, qua cc phin gp, vo cc thanh dn ca dy qun phn ng nm trong t trng $\Phi$ ca cc cc t chnh, ri qua cc phin gp khc v ra chi than m.
  • Theo Quy tc bn tay tri, cc thanh dn mang dng in $I_a$ nm di cc cc t khc nhau (N v S) s chu tc dng ca cc lc in t $F_e$ c chiu ngc nhau. Cp lc ny to thnh mt moment quay (Torque), lm cho rotor (phn ng) quay.
  • Vai tr ca C gp: Tng t nh my pht, c gp ng vai tr cc k quan trng. Khi rotor quay, c gp s i ni cc thanh dn vi chi than sao cho cc thanh dn nm di mt cc t nht nh lun c dng in chy theo mt chiu xc nh (v d: lun i vo di cc N, lun i ra di cc S). iu ny m bo rng chiu ca lc in t tc dng ln cc thanh dn di mi cc t khng i, v do , moment quay tc dng ln rotor lun theo mt chiu, gip ng c quay lin tc. Nu khng c c gp (dng vnh trt), moment s i chiu sau mi na vng quay v ng c khng th quay lin tc c.
  • Sc phn in ng (Back EMF / Counter EMF - CEMF): Khi rotor ca ng c quay trong t trng, cc thanh dn ca dy qun phn ng cng ct cc ng sc t. Theo Quy tc bn tay phi (nguyn l my pht), mt sc in ng $E_a$ s c cm ng trong dy qun phn ng. Sc in ng ny c chiu ngc li vi chiu in p t vo $V_t$ (theo nh lut Lenz) nn c gi l sc phn in ng.
  • ngha ca $E_a$: Sc phn in ng $E_a$ ng vai tr t iu chnh tc ca ng c. N ph thuc vo t thng $\Phi$ v tc quay $\omega$ ($E_a = k\Phi\omega$). Phng trnh cn bng in p ca phn ng ng c l: $ V_t = E_a + I_a R_a $
    Trong $I_a R_a$ l st p trn in tr mch phn ng ($R_a$ bao gm in tr dy qun phn ng v in tr tip xc chi than). T y suy ra dng in phn ng: $ I_a = \frac{V_t - E_a}{R_a} $ Khi ng c mi khi ng, tc $\omega \approx 0$ nn $E_a \approx 0$. Do , dng in khi ng $I_a = V_t / R_a$ thng rt ln (v $R_a$ nh). Khi tc ng c tng ln, $E_a$ tng theo, lm cho hiu s $(V_t - E_a)$ gim xung, dn n dng $I_a$ gim xung n mt gi tr cn bng, ph hp vi moment ti c t ln trc ng c.

6. Phn ng phn ng (Armature Reaction)

  • Khi my in mt chiu lm vic c ti, dng in $I_a$ chy trong dy qun phn ng. Dng in ny cng to ra mt t trng ring ca n, gi l t trng phn ng hay phn ng phn ng.
  • T trng phn ng ny vung gc (ngang trc - quadrature axis) vi t trng chnh do cc cc t kch t to ra (trc dc - direct axis), khi chi than c t v tr trung tnh hnh hc.
  • S tng tc: T trng phn ng s tng tc vi t trng kch t chnh, lm cho t trng tng trong khe h khng kh ca my b bin dng so vi khi khng ti ($I_a = 0$).
  • Hu qu ca Phn ng phn ng:
    1. Mo dng t trng: T trng tng b yu i mp cc t ny v mnh ln mp cc t kia (theo chiu quay). iu ny lm mt t thng phn b khng u di mt cc t.
    2. Dch chuyn mt trung tnh: Mt phng trung tnh hnh hc (ni l tng t chi than khi khng ti, v ti s trong cc phn t i chiu bng 0) b lch i mt gc so vi v tr ban u. Mt trung tnh thc t (ni t trng tng bng 0) s b lch theo chiu quay i vi ng c v ngc chiu quay i vi my pht. Nu chi than khng c t ng v tr mt trung tnh thc t, qu trnh i chiu dng in trong cc phn t dy qun ni vi phin gp s kh khn, gy ra tia la in gia chi than v c gp, lm mn nhanh v c th h hng my.
    3. Hiu ng kh t: Thnh phn t trng ngang trc ca phn ng, khi xt n s bo ha ca mch t, c th gy ra hiu ng lm suy gim t thng chnh $\Phi$ ca my. Mc suy gim ny tng ln khi dng ti $I_a$ tng. Vic t thng b suy gim s nh hng trc tip n sc in ng $E_a$ v moment $T_{dt}$ ca my ($E_a = k\Phi\omega$, $T_{dt} = k\Phi I_a$).
  • Khc phc: gim thiu tc hi ca phn ng phn ng, ngi ta thng s dng:
    • Cc t ph (Interpoles): To ra t trng ph kh t trng phn ng ti vng chi than, ci thin i chiu.
    • Cun b (Compensating Windings): t trong rnh trn mt cc t chnh, to ra t trng ngc chiu v trit tiu t trng phn ng ngay di mt cc t.

7. Cc t ph (Interpoles) v Cun b (Compensating Windings)

Phn ng phn ng gy ra hai vn chnh: lm mo dng t trng di mt cc t chnh v lm lch mt trung tnh gy kh khn cho qu trnh i chiu (nh la chi than). khc phc cc vn ny, c bit trong cc my in DC cng sut ln hoc yu cu cht lng i chiu cao, ngi ta s dng cc t ph v/hoc cun b.

  • a. Cc t ph (Interpoles / Commutating Poles):

    • Cu to: L cc cc t nh t xen k gia cc cc t chnh. Dy qun ca cc cc t ph c s vng t nhng tit din ln, c ni ni tip vi dy qun phn ng (v cun b nu c).
    • Nguyn l hot ng: Mc ch chnh ca cc t ph l ci thin qu trnh i chiu (commutation). Khi dng in phn ng $I_a$ chy qua dy qun cc t ph, n to ra mt t trng ph $\Phi_{ctp}$ ti vng chi than (vng i chiu).
    • Phn cc: Cc tnh ca cc t ph c thit k sao cho t trng $\Phi_{ctp}$ n to ra:
      • Chng li t trng do phn ng phn ng gy ra ti vng i chiu.
      • Cm ng mt sc in ng i chiu ($e_{rot}$) trong cc phn t dy qun ang i chiu. Sc in ng ny c chiu ngc li vi sc in ng t cm ($e_{L} = -L \frac{di}{dt}$) sinh ra do s bin thin nhanh ca dng in trong phn t khi i chiu.
      • Quy tc xc nh cc tnh:
        • My pht: Cc t ph c cc tnh ging cc t chnh tip theo theo chiu quay.
        • ng c: Cc t ph c cc tnh ging cc t chnh pha sau n theo chiu quay (hoc ngc vi cc t chnh tip theo).
    • u im: V dy qun cc t ph ni ni tip vi phn ng, t trng $\Phi_{ctp}$ n to ra t l vi dng $I_a$. iu ny rt c li v sc in ng t cm $e_L$ cng t l vi $I_a$. Do , cc t ph c th t ng to ra s i chiu ph hp vi nhiu mc ti khc nhau, gip qu trnh i chiu din ra tt hn v gim ng k tia la in chi than. Hu ht cc my in DC hin i (tr cc my rt nh) u c cc t ph.

  • b. Cun b (Compensating Windings):

    • Cu to: L cc thanh dn hoc cun dy t trong cc rnh x trn b mt ca cc cc t chnh. Cun b cng c ni ni tip vi dy qun phn ng.
    • Nguyn l hot ng: Mc ch chnh ca cun b l chng li trc tip nh hng ca t trng phn ng ngay di mt cc cc t chnh. Dng in $I_a$ chy qua cun b theo chiu ngc li vi chiu dng in trong cc thanh dn phn ng i din n. Do , t trng do cun b to ra s gn nh trit tiu t trng ngang trc do phn ng gy ra trong vng di mt cc t.
    • Tc dng: Vic ny gip:
      • Gim s mo dng ca t trng chnh.
      • Ngn chn s bo ha cc b cc mp cc t khi ti ln hoc thay i t ngt.
      • Gi cho s phn b t thng di mt cc t n nh hn.
    • ng dng: Cun b c cu to phc tp v t tin hn nn thng ch c s dng trong cc my in DC cng sut rt ln hoc cc my lm vic ch ti nng, thay i t ngt (nh ng c my cn thp) m bo s n nh v cht lng lm vic cao.

II. Phn tch My pht mt chiu

II.1. Th nghim khng ti v c tnh khng ti (OCC)

  • a. Mc ch:

    • Th nghim khng ti (Open-Circuit Test) c thc hin xc nh mi quan h gia sc in ng cm ng trong phn ng ($E_a$) v dng in kch t ($I_f$) khi my pht quay tc khng i (thng l tc nh mc $n_{dm}$) v mch phn ng h (khng ni ti, $I_a = 0$).
    • Mi quan h ny $E_a = f(I_f)$ ti $n = n_{dm} = const$ c gi l c tnh khng ti hay ng cong t ha (Open-Circuit Characteristic - OCC, hoc Magnetization Curve). ng cong ny th hin c tnh t ca my.

  • b. Cch tin hnh:

    1. Ni mch: Ni mch kch t vi mt ngun DC c th iu chnh c (v d: qua bin tr) thay i $I_f$. Mch phn ng h, ni mt Vn k c in tr ln vo hai u cc phn ng o in p $V_t$.
    2. Chy my: Dng mt ng c s cp (prime mover) ko my pht quay tc khng i, thng l tc nh mc $n_{dm}$.
    3. o c: Tng dn dng kch t $I_f$ t 0 n mt gi tr ln hn nh mc mt cht. Ti mi gi tr $I_f$, ghi li gi tr in p u cc $V_t$. Do mch phn ng h ($I_a = 0$), st p $I_a R_a = 0$, nn in p o c chnh l sc in ng cm ng: $V_t = E_a$.
    4. C th gim dn $I_f$ v 0 v ghi li gi tr $E_a$ tng ng thy r hin tng t d.

  • c. ng cong OCC $E_a = f(I_f)$:

    • th v mi quan h gia $E_a$ (trc tung) v $I_f$ (trc honh) thu c t th nghim l ng cong OCC.
    • c im ca OCC:
      • T d: Khi $I_f = 0$, thng $E_a > 0$ do trong li thp vn cn t d. on $E_a$ ny cn thit cho qu trnh t kch ca my pht t kch.
      • on tuyn tnh (Air-gap line): Khi $I_f$ cn nh, mch t cha bo ha, t thng $\Phi$ t l gn nh tuyn tnh vi $I_f$. V $E_a = k\Phi\omega$ v $\omega$ khng i, nn $E_a$ cng t l gn nh tuyn tnh vi $I_f$. on ny cn gi l ng c tnh khe h khng kh.
      • on cong (Bo ha): Khi $I_f$ tng ln, mch t bt u bo ha. Mc d $I_f$ tip tc tng, nhng $\Phi$ tng chm li, do $E_a$ cng tng chm li v ng cong b un cong i.
    • nh hng ca tc : V $E_a = k\Phi\omega$, nu th nghim c thc hin tc $n'$ khc vi tc $n_{dm}$ m OCC c v, th gi tr $E_a'$ ti tc $n'$ s l: $$ E_a'(n') = E_a(n_{dm}) \times \frac{n'}{n_{dm}} $$ (vi cng mt gi tr $I_f$, v $\Phi$ ch ph thuc $I_f$).

  • d. Biu din v s dng OCC trong tnh ton (Python):

    • Lu tr d liu: D liu OCC (cc cp im $(I_f, E_a)$ o c ti $n_{dm}$) thng c lu tr di dng hai mng (list hoc numpy array).
    • Ni suy: Do khng th o v s im, khi cn tm $E_a$ ti mt gi tr $I_f$ khng c trong bng hoc ngc li, ta dng phng php ni suy tuyn tnh gia hai im d liu gn nht. Th vin numpy cung cp hm np.interp() rt tin li cho vic ny.
    • Hm Python: Cc hm get_Ea_from_If v get_If_from_Ea s dng d liu OCC.

II.2. My pht DC kch t c lp (Separately Excited DC Generator)

  • a. S mch v Nguyn l:

    • y l loi my pht m mch kch t c cp ngun bi mt ngun DC bn ngoi, hon ton c lp vi mch phn ng ca my pht.
    • Mch kch t: Gm ngun $V_f$ v tng in tr mch kch t $R_f$ (bao gm in tr cun dy $R_{f\_coil}$ v bin tr iu chnh $R_{adj}$ nu c). Dng kch t c xc nh bi: $$ I_f = \frac{V_f}{R_f} $$
    • Mch phn ng: Khi my pht quay bi ng c s cp vi tc gc $\omega$, sc in ng $E_a$ c cm ng trong dy qun phn ng. $E_a$ ph thuc vo t thng $\Phi$ (do $I_f$ to ra) v tc $\omega$: $$ E_a = k \Phi \omega $$ (Trong $k$ l hng s cu to ca my). Nu mch t cha bo ha, $\Phi$ t l vi $I_f$, nn $E_a \approx k' I_f \omega$. Nu mch t bo ha, mi quan h $E_a = f(I_f)$ ti mt tc $\omega_0$ c xc nh bi ng cong OCC.
    • Khi ni mch phn ng vi ti $R_{load}$, dng in phn ng $I_a$ chy ra ti. Do kch t c lp, dng ti $I_L$ chnh bng dng phn ng: $I_L = I_a$.
    • Phng trnh cn bng in p: in p u cc $V_t$ cp cho ti c xc nh bi: $$ V_t = E_a - I_a R_a $$ Trong $R_a$ l tng in tr mch phn ng (bao gm in tr dy qun phn ng, in tr tip xc chi than, in tr cc t ph v cun b nu c).

  • b. c tnh (External Characteristic):

    • c tnh biu din mi quan h gia in p u cc $V_t$ v dng in ti $I_L$ (hay $I_a$) khi tc quay $\omega$ v dng kch t $I_f$ (do c t thng $\Phi$) c gi khng i: $V_t = f(I_L)$ vi $\omega=const, I_f=const$.
    • T phng trnh $V_t = E_a - I_a R_a$, v $E_a = k\Phi\omega$ l hng s trong iu kin ny, nn $V_t$ gim tuyn tnh khi $I_a$ tng.
    • $V_t = E_a|_{I_f, \omega} - R_a I_L$
    • y l mt ng thng dc xung. dc ca ng c tnh ngoi ph thuc vo in tr phn ng $R_a$. $R_a$ cng nh, c tnh cng "cng" (t dc).
    • Nu xt thm nh hng ca phn ng phn ng (lm gim $\Phi$ khi $I_a$ tng), th $E_a$ s gim nh khi $I_a$ tng, lm cho ng c tnh ngoi thc t hi cong xung v dc hn so vi ng thng l thuyt.

  • c. Gin cng sut:

    • Cng sut c vo: $P_{co} = T_{co} \omega$ (Cng sut t ng c s cp)
    • Tn hao quay: $P_{rot}$ (Tn hao c do ma st, qut gi + Tn hao st t trong li phn ng)
    • Cng sut in t: $P_{dt} = P_{co} - P_{rot} = E_a I_a$ (Cng sut chuyn i t c sang in trong phn ng)
    • Tn hao ng phn ng: $P_{Cu,a} = I_a^2 R_a$
    • Cng sut in u ra (ra ti): $P_{out} = P_{dt} - P_{Cu,a} = V_t I_L = V_t I_a$
    • Cng sut kch t (t ngun ring): $P_f = V_f I_f = I_f^2 R_f$
    • Tng cng sut vo: $P_{in} = P_{co} + P_f$
    • Hiu sut: $\eta = \frac{P_{out}}{P_{in}} = \frac{P_{out}}{P_{co} + P_f}$

  • d. Hm Python tnh ton (V d cho trng hp tuyn tnh hoc bit Ea):
